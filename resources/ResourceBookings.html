Reserve a Space

Book a hot desk, office, or meeting room in a few quick steps.

Step-by-Step Instructions

1
Go to SGF.work New Tab
Or open a new tab — it'll take you there automatically on your @members.sgf.co chrome profile.
2
Click "Book a Room"
Found in the bottom navigation bar of the new tab page.
3
Google Calendar will open
You'll see the weekly calendar view for your member account.
4
Confirm you're signed in as your member account
Check the top right — it should show your @members.sgf.co address, managed by gwh.co.
5
Click "+ Create"
The blue button in the top-left corner of Google Calendar.
6
Select "Event"
Choose Event from the dropdown — not Task or Reminder.
7
Type your event name
Your name, company, or what you're working on — e.g. "Sean — Deep Work".
8
Set your start time
Click the time field and select when you plan to arrive.
9
Set your end time
Select when you'll wrap up.
10
Confirm your time window looks correct
You can also adjust via the Duration field if needed.
11
Click "Add rooms or location"
This is where you pick the actual space you want to reserve.
12
Click "Add rooms" → "Browse all rooms & resources"
This shows the full list of available spaces at SGF.work.
13
Select "SGF Flagship 01"
This is our main location.
14
Choose your space
Pick the specific desk or room — e.g. Hot Desk 1. Only available spaces will appear.
15
Click "Go back" to return to the event
Your selected room will now be attached to the event.
16
Review your booking details
Confirm the time, room, and name all look correct.
17
Click "Save" — you're booked! 🎉
The space is reserved under your member account and will appear on your calendar.
💡 Unavailable spaces will be crossed out when browsing rooms. If a desk is already taken during your time window, it will be crossed out.

Need Help?

If you run into any issues booking a space, reach out at hello@sgf.work or stop by the front desk.
